Cream Paletas; Our ice cream bars are traditional paletas made with fresh cream and real fruit. Try All Our Creamy Flavor. A Milkshake; is a sweet beverage made by blending milk, ice cream, caramel sauce, chocolate syrup, fruit syrup, or whole fruit into a thick, sweet, cold mixture. Exceptional ice cream; is typically made from milk or cream and flavored with a sweetener, either sugar or an alternative, and spice, such as cocoa or vanilla, or with fruit such as strawberries or peaches. Scoops and cones are available at area ice cream shops. An ice cream popsicle; is a frozen dessert on a stick or a candy bar with ice cream. The coating is usually a thin layer of chocolate used to prevent the melting and dripping of ice cream.
